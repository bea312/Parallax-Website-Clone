ABOUT LAKE KIVU

A paradise in the heart of Africa

Lake Kivu is one of the African Great Lakes, located on the border between Rwanda and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. Known for its emerald-green waters and surrounded by magnificent mountains, it is the perfect destination for relaxation and adventure. Unlike many other African lakes, Kivu is safe for swimming as it contains no hippos or crocodiles. Visitors can enjoy serene boat rides, explore the many lush islands, or simply relax on the sandy shores of Rubavu.

Western Rwanda

Welcome to the shores of Lake Kivu. Whether you are looking for a quiet retreat or an active holiday, this region has it all. From the vibrant lakeside towns to the hidden gems of the many islands, Lake Kivu offers a unique experience that combines natural beauty with warm hospitality. You can spend your days kayaking, visiting local coffee plantations, or watching the sunset over the hills of the Congo. It is a place where nature meets tranquility, providing a truly unforgettable escape.
